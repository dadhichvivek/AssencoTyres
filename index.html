About MTPL

Ascenso is owned by the Mahansaria Tyres Private Limited (MTPL). The company will cater to three key global segments: Agriculture, Industrial & Construction and Earth Mover. With an investment of over US$ 100 million, MTPL has set up a 127-acre greenfield off-highway tyres plant in Gujarat, India with a manufacturing capacity of 70,000 MT built in two phases.
